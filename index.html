How to Play Stack Fall

Master these key elements to become a Stack Fall champion!

1

Understand Controls

Tap and hold the screen to make your ball fall. Release to stop falling. Timing is crucial for success!

2

Master Color Matching

Break through platforms of matching colors. Your ball will shatter if it hits mismatched colors.

3

Build Up Fever Mode

Successfully breaking platforms fills your fever gauge. Activate it to smash through all platforms temporarily!

4

Avoid Black Platforms

Black platforms are unbreakable and will destroy your ball on contact. Time your stops carefully!

5

Progress Through Levels

Each level presents new platform patterns and challenges. Master them all to become a Stack Fall expert!

6

Beat High Scores

Challenge yourself to break more platforms and achieve higher scores with each attempt!

Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions About Stack Fall Gameplay

How to play Stack Fall?

In Stack Fall, you control a falling ball that breaks through colored platforms. Tap to make the ball fall and release to stop. Match colors to progress and avoid black platforms to survive.

What are the different platform colors?

Stack Fall features colored platforms that match your ball's color which you can break through, and black platforms that must be avoided as they will destroy your ball on contact.

What platforms can I play Stack Fall on?

Stack Fall is available on web browsers and can be played on both desktop and mobile devices.

How does fever mode work?

Fever mode activates when you fill up the gauge by successfully breaking platforms. When active, your ball can smash through all platforms regardless of their color.

When was Stack Fall released?

Stack Fall was released on January 3, 2025.
